Man City go 12 points clear, Spurs stunned by Southampton

Manchester City surged 12 points clear at The top of the Premier League with a 2-0 win against Brentford,
while Tottenham's top four bid suffered a blow in a dramatic 3-2 defeat against Southampton on Wednesday.

City were held to a 1-1 draw at Southampton in their previous league game,
but they got back on track to pile the pressure on second placed Liverpool,
who host Leicester on Thursday in one of their two games in hand on the champions
